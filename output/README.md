# output

Generated analysis results and research documents produced by the trading system's optimization and analysis tools.

## Files

### optimized_strategy_parameters.json

Per-cryptocurrency optimized strategy parameters generated by `sma-prediction/multi_cryptocurrency_optimizer.py`. Contains entries for 24 cryptocurrencies, each with:

- SMA window sizes (short and long)
- Regime detection thresholds (slope, BB width)
- Bollinger Band parameters (window, standard deviations)
- Mean reversion settings (entry threshold, exit ratio)
- Backtested performance metrics (return %, Sharpe ratio, Sortino ratio, Calmar ratio, win rate, max drawdown, trade count)

These parameters are loaded at runtime by `trading_strategy.py:make_optimized_trading_decision()` to configure per-asset strategy behavior.

### force_currency_allocation.json

Portfolio allocation across 16 selected cryptocurrencies with USD amounts per position. Generated by the optimizer based on backtested risk-adjusted performance. Used by the trading bot for position sizing.

### research_report.md

Architecture analysis of the trading bot's current state, limitations, and future improvement directions. Covers the dual-mode adaptive strategy (SMA crossover + Bollinger Band mean reversion), regime detection mechanics, and identified gaps in signal coverage.

### research_report_v2.md

Updated architecture analysis (v2) incorporating decision logs. Documents the rationale for prioritizing feature engineering over ML/RL approaches. Expands on signal pipeline architecture, including the multi-signal composite scoring design.

### cluster_sentiment_research.md

Research foundation for sector-based sentiment analysis. Surveys existing methods for clustering assets by sector and routing news sentiment to sector-specific trading signals. Covers the QuantConnect sector rotation approach, academic literature on crypto co-movement, and the design rationale for the 6-sector taxonomy (L1 infra, DeFi, AI/compute, meme, store of value, other) used in the sentiment pipeline.
